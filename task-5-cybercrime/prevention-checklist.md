Stay Safe Online – Checklist (for College Students in India)
	•	Use strong, unique passwords for each account; enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
	•	Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details over calls, messages, or emails.
	•	Verify links before clicking—avoid unknown or shortened URLs.
	•	Keep your devices updated with antivirus and the latest software patches.
	•	Adjust privacy settings on social media; avoid oversharing personal information.
	•	Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or important accounts.
	•	UPI Safety: Always verify the recipient’s name before approving payments.
	•	UPI Safety: Never approve “collect requests” from unknown sources—money is deducted when you approve.
	•	Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public systems.
	•	Report suspicious activity immediately via the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or Helpline 1930.
