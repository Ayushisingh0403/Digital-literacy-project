📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students)

✅ Do’s
	•	Use a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments
	•	Keep your profiles clean and appropriate for academic and career purposes
	•	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news
	•	Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your content
	•	Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development

⸻

❌ Don’ts
	•	Do not share personal or sensitive information (phone number, address, passwords)
	•	Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
	•	Do not engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying
	•	Avoid oversharing private life details or emotional posts
	•	Do not upload content that can damage your reputation or future career
